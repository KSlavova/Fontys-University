Guess The Word Game - Manual Page

Overview:

Welcome to Guess The Word Game! This game is a new represantation of The Hangman game, that suitable for young children. It will help your kid develop their writing skills while having the time of their life.

How to play:

  1. Every game starts with a hint that helps you to guess the word.
  2. You have two balloons(two tries) at each start.
  3. For each correct letter, you earn a balloon.
  4. For each wrong letter, you lose a balloon.
  5. The game ends when you guess the word or when you lose all your balloons(run out of attempts).

Game rules:

  1. You can only guess one letter at a time.
  2. If a letter repeats itself, you get only one balloon and the letter will automatically appear on its other places.
  3. If you guess the word before running out of attempts, you win.
  4. If you run out of attempts, you lose.

Tips and tricks:

  • Always read the hints. They are there to navigate you.
  • Always start by guessing vowels.
  • Enjoy the game and don't be affraid to lose, because this is the only right way to become a winner.
